Krabby Patty Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of peeled, deveined shrimp
  • 1 egg
  • 10-12 regular-sized or smaller hamburger buns
  • Lemon zest to taste
  • Old Bay seasoning
  • 1 Tbsp of our favorite hot sauce (I use Crystal)
  • 1 tsp celery seed
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Vegetable oil and/or butter for frying

Steps

  1. Put everything into a food processor
  2. When everything is a paste, make patties. Should get about 8
  3. Fry and assemble
  4. Add onions, lettuce, tomatoes, and sandwich between hamburger buns

Serve!!!
